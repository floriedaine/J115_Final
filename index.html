a bit about me

My name is Floriedaine Paran, and I am a Filipinx- American-- born and raised in the Bay Area. I am a first-generation college student at the University of California, Berkeley where I'm majoring in Media Studies with a minor in Journalism. Some of my interests include filmmaking, photography, writing, and discovering. My dream is to enter the film industry, but in the meantime, I work on personal film projects like the "Gemini Project", presented below. I believe I am still in the process of exploring and forming my identity. My potential is infinite.
